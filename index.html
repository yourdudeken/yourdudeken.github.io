    About

    A bit about me

    Full-stack developer advocating for responsive, accessible, and inclusive digital products.

    I'm Kennedy Muthengi, a full-stack developer based in Nairobi. I build web platforms, fintech tools, and developer APIs — with a particular interest in payments, accessibility, and products built for African markets.

    My recent work includes EventTik, a ticketing and event platform with M-Pesa built in, and several open-source projects exploring AI-assisted tooling and self-hosted software. I care about clean architecture, fast load times, and interfaces that anyone can use.
